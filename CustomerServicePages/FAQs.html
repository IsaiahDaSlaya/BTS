BTS FAQs!

FAQ

How can I return my order?

For any reason, you may return your order to BTS Clothing within 30 days of the purchase date by visiting the returns page. Please review the terms and conditions on our return page for additional information about returning goods. Depending on the reason for return you may be responsible for shipping and handling charges.

How do I exchange my order?

We do not offer direct exchanges. If you would like to exchange goods, we ask that you simply place a new order for the replacement goods and visit the returns page within 30 days of your original purchase to return the goods you would like to return.

How do I cancel or change my order?

As soon as you place your order we begin processing it. Therefore, we are unable to change or cancel orders. Please visit the returns page to initiate a return if you wish to return goods. The terms and conditions for returns will apply.

I did not receive all the goods I ordered or received the wrong goods.

Please visit the returns / customer service page and we will remedy the issue immediately.

Are your goods first quality?

All products we carry and sell are “first quality” and free of major defects.

Do you accept back orders?

We do not accept orders for inventory we do not have, so we do not accept back orders.

Is there a minimum order size?

We do not have a minimum order size.

How do I return defective goods?

You may return your order to BTS Clothing within 72 hours of receiving the order if they are defective. You will receive replacement goods via the same shipping service you chose during check out. Please report claims using the returns page.

How long will it take me to get my order?

Estimated delivery dates are provided at check out and are not guaranteed. When available you will be provided tracking information.

How can I check the status of my order?

Click on the link to My Account in the upper left corner of the page or click here.

How can I ship my order?

Upon check out you can select between various ground and air options. We will select the carrier to provide you the best value and service based on your location.

Can you ship internationally or process international credit cards?

We do not currently offer international shipping and can’t accept credit cards from outside of the United States.

What if I order something and it is out of stock?

We will remove the out of stock items from your order ship the rest of your order. You will not be charged for the out of stock items.

We are a school or government organization, do you accept purchase orders?

Please contact BTSclothing@gmail for additional information.

Who do I contact if I need other help?

Our friendly customer service team can be contacted by emailing BTSclothing@gmail. You can expect a response within 48 business hours. For returns please visit the returns page.
